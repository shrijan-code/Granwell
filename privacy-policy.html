1. Who we are

Granwell ("Granwell", "we", "us", "our") is a Support at Home provider based in Canberra, ACT. This policy explains how we handle personal information we collect through this website, by email, by phone, or while providing care services.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details you give us — name, email address, phone number
  • Information you share when enquiring about or receiving care services, including details relevant to your care needs
  • Correspondence between you and Granwell
  • Basic technical information from visiting this website (such as browser type), where applicable

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the services you request
  • To coordinate and deliver care, including with other providers you've asked us to work with
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations, including reporting to the Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission (ACQSC) where required
  • To send you information relevant to your care, where appropriate

4. Disclosure of information

We don't sell personal information. We may share information with other health or care providers involved in your support (with your consent), with regulatory bodies where legally required, or with service providers who help us operate (such as software providers), under confidentiality obligations.

5. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, using appropriate physical and electronic safeguards. [Describe specific storage systems / practices once finalised.]

6. Access and correction

You can ask to access or correct the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us at info@granwell.com.au.

7. Complaints about privacy

If you have a concern about how we've handled your personal information, contact us at complaints@granwell.com.au. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.
