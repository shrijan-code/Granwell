Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, store and protect your personal information.

This notice is based on Granwell's Privacy Collection Notice and registration documentation. Last reviewed: [ADD: date] — please confirm with your privacy/legal advisor before treating this as final, particularly the recipients table in section 4, which should be checked against Granwell's actual current arrangements.

1. Who we are

Granwell ("Granwell", "we", "us", "our") is a Support at Home provider registered in Canberra, ACT, providing services to older Australians nationally. This notice explains how we handle personal information we collect through this website, by email, by phone, or while providing care services.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details you give us — name, email address, phone number, postal address
  • Your My Aged Care reference number, where relevant to arranging or coordinating your services
  • Your Medicare number, where relevant to your care funding or claims
  • Information you share when enquiring about or receiving care services, including details relevant to your health and care needs
  • Details of any associated providers already involved in your care, so we can coordinate with them
  • Correspondence between you and Granwell
  • Basic technical information from visiting this website (such as browser type), where applicable

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the services you request
  • To coordinate and deliver care, including with other providers you've asked us to work with
  • To manage funding and claims relevant to your Support at Home services
  • To meet our legal and regulatory obligations, including reporting to the Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission (ACQSC) where required
  • To send you information relevant to your care, where appropriate

4. Who we share information with

We don't sell personal information. Depending on the services you use, we may share relevant information with:

RecipientPurpose
My Aged Care / Department of Health, Disability and AgeingEligibility, funding and program administration
Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission (ACQSC)Regulatory reporting and compliance
Associated or subcontracted care providers involved in your supportDelivering the specific services in your care plan
Services Australia (Medicare)Where relevant to your care funding or claims
IT and software service providersOperating our booking, records and communication systems

We only share what's needed for the purpose, and require anyone we share information with to keep it confidential. Where we use service providers based overseas (for example, cloud hosting or software providers), we take reasonable steps to ensure your information continues to be handled in line with the Australian Privacy Principles.

5. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, using appropriate physical and electronic safeguards. [Describe specific storage systems / practices once finalised.]

6. Access and correction

You can ask to access or correct the personal information we hold about you at any time by contacting us at info [at] granwell [dot] com [dot] au.

7. Complaints about privacy

If you have a concern about how we've handled your personal information, contact us at complaints@granwell.com.au. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au. You can also reach the Older Persons Advocacy Network (OPAN) on 1800 700 600 for free, independent support with any concern, privacy-related or otherwise.
