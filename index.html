What we do

Three kinds of help, one provider.

Support at Home groups services into categories. Here's what that means for you in plain terms — and what Granwell delivers.

Category 1

At home, day to day

The practical things that keep a home running and a person fed and mobile.

  • Domestic assistance
  • Home maintenance & minor repairs
  • Meals
  • Transport
Category 3

Staying connected

Support for the things that keep a home liveable and a person part of their community.

  • Social support & community engagement
  • Hoarding & squalor assistance
Category 4

Care that's organised for you

Your care partner keeps the whole picture in view — your budget, your providers, your changing needs — so you're not the one holding it all together.

  • Care management
  • Coordinating your services
  • Reviewing your care plan as things change
Getting started

How it works

Get in touch

Email us or send a message. We'll ask a few questions about your situation and what kind of support you're after.

We build your plan together

If you're eligible through My Aged Care, we'll help you understand your funding and put together a service agreement that fits.

We coordinate, you live your life

Your care partner arranges the services, checks in regularly, and adjusts things as your needs change.

About Granwell

A new provider, built around one idea.

Granwell was set up to do one thing well: help older people in Canberra stay in their own home for longer, with less of the burden falling on them or their family to coordinate it all.

We're a small, local team — which means when you call, you talk to someone who actually knows your situation, not a call centre.

Granwell is currently completing its registration with the Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission (ACQSC) as a Support at Home provider. We'll update this page as our registration progresses.
