What we do

Three kinds of help, one provider.

Support at Home groups services into categories. Here's what that means for you in plain terms — and what Granwell delivers.

Category 1

At home, day to day

The practical things that keep a home running and a person fed and mobile.

  • Domestic assistance
  • Home maintenance & minor repairs
  • Meals
  • Transport

About Granwell

A new provider, built around one idea.

Granwell was set up to do one thing well: help older people across Australia stay in their own home for longer, with less of the burden falling on them or their family to coordinate it all.

We're a small, local team — which means when you call, you talk to someone who actually knows your situation, not a call centre.

Common questions

Before you get in touch

Am I eligible for Support at Home?

Eligibility is assessed through My Aged Care. If you're not already assessed, we can point you to the right first step — get in touch and we'll walk you through it.

What does Support at Home cost?

Costs depend on your assessed funding level and any income-tested contribution set by Services Australia. We'll go through what applies to your situation before you commit to anything.

Can I switch to Granwell from my current provider?

Yes. Changing Support at Home providers is a normal, supported process. We'll help you understand the steps and timing so the switch is smooth.
