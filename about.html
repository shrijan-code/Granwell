About Granwell

A new provider, built around one idea.

Granwell was set up to do one thing well: help older people in Australia stay in their own home for longer, with less of the burden falling on them or their family to coordinate it all.

Our approach

Local, personal, and genuinely accountable.

We're a small, local team — which means when you call, you talk to someone who actually knows your situation, not a call centre reading from a script.

We believe good home care isn't about doing more things to a person — it's about organising the right things around them, so they keep as much control over their own life as possible.

Granwell is currently completing its registration with the Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission (ACQSC) as a Support at Home provider. We'll update this page as our registration progresses.
